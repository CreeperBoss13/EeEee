Movement 1 Drill Preview

Brendan Grzanic 8/31/2023 5:19 PM

2023 CHS Marching Band - Below is a link to a computer generated preview of our 1st movement drill rendered from an audience perspective. I would encourage everyone to watch this once or twice for some additional perspective on both what your individual role is in the various forms we create as well as observing the other structures that are taking shape on the field that you may not normally be able to see.

Our drill sheets provide the blueprint for various snapshots in time, but like a great musical performance, the artistry is what happens "between the notes."
